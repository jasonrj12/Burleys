Our Story

From Island Spice to Crispy, Golden Joy

Burley's began with a simple promise: take the boldness of Sri Lankan flavour — fragrant Ceylon spices, lime, garlic, curry leaves — and build a modern fried chicken experience around it. No shortcuts. No compromise. Just craveable crunch with soul.

  • Signature Ceylon spice blend inspired by island kitchens
  • Fried-to-order for peak crunch
  • Community-first mindset — sourcing & hiring locally

Burley's is a 100% Halal, Sri Lanka based High-End Fast Food Restaurant founded in 2020, It was a huge transition for us from the Fashion Industry to the Food Industry with no experience at all, We've been testing out recipes & planning menus ever since and it was after much hard work and anticipation that Burley's Officially Opened its doors to the public on the 21st of February, 2022, The menu at Burley's was carefully curated aiming to bring a new level of flavour and quality, To Our Customers. It was merely the passion we had for food that turned into a flaming desire that turned into reality...
